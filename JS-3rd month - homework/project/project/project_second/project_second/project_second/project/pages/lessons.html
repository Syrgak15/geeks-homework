Mastering Bread Baking
Get ready to knead, proof, and bake your way to artisanal bread perfection in this comprehensive master class with Master Baker Sophia Nguyen. Over the course of two dynamic sessions, you'll delve into the science and techniques behind crafting bakery-quality loaves, pastries, and rolls from scratch. From classic sourdough to flavored focaccia, you'll learn expert tips for mixing doughs, shaping, and achieving perfect baking results every time.
Price : 300$
Plating like a Pro
Elevate your presentation skills with this master class led by award-winning Chef Marco Rossi. Explore the art of plating and learn how to transform your dishes into edible works of art. Through live demos and one-on-one guidance, you'll master fundamental techniques like balancing flavors, playing with textures, and leveraging color and height. Discover how small details can make a big impact on the overall visual appeal of your plates.

Here are some facts about our culinary center

World-Class Instruction

The Epicurean Arts Culinary Center is renowned for its exceptional faculty of acclaimed chefs and industry leaders. Students learn from culinary masters who bring decades of experience and a passion for passing on their expertise.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

The center boasts cutting-edge kitchen laboratories, demonstration studios, and dedicated baking labs, all equipped with the latest commercial-grade appliances and tools. This allows students to develop their skills in a professional, hands-on environment.

Diverse Curriculum

The curriculum covers a comprehensive range of culinary disciplines, from classic French techniques to global cuisine and modern food science. Students graduate with a well-rounded skill set to succeed in any culinary role

Unparalleled Industry Connections

Through extensive industry partnerships, the Epicurean Arts Culinary Center provides students with invaluable networking opportunities and real-world experiential learning. Many graduates go on to secure top positions at prestigious restaurants and food companies..

Commitment to Sustainability

Sustainability and ethical sourcing are core values at the Epicurean Arts Culinary Center. Students learn about sustainable farming practices, waste reduction, and the importance of using locally-sourced, seasonal ingredients in their cooking..
