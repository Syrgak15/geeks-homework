EACC Online Learning
The Epicurean Arts Culinary Center offers an immersive online learning experience, bringing the expertise of our renowned chefs directly to your kitchen. Explore a world of culinary techniques, from classical French cuisine to innovative global flavors. Our interactive virtual classes allow you to hone your skills and expand your culinary horizons, no matter your location. Elevate your cooking with the guidance of industry-leading instructors and connect with a passionate community of food enthusiasts. Discover the joy of the culinary arts through Epicurean Arts' online learning programs.
Choose the program best suited to your ambition.
Introduction to Culinary Arts Fundamentals
This course will cover the basic techniques and principles of food preparation, such as cutting, simmering, sautéing, and baking. Students will gain the fundamental knowledge necessary to create delicious and well-balanced dishes.

Duration:4 weeks

Delivery Schedule:02 - 30 August 2024

Details:This comprehensive course will provide students with a solid foundation in the essential techniques and principles of food preparation. Participants will learn fundamental knife skills, such as dicing, slicing, and mincing, as well as mastering various cooking methods, including sautéing and baking

World Cuisines: A Culinary Journey
This course will explore the culinary traditions of various regions and countries, from Mediterranean cuisine to Asian kitchens. Students will learn about the ingredients, cooking methods, and presentation styles unique to each culture.

Duration:10 Weeks

Delivery Schedule:08 September-16 November 2024

Details:Students will learn about the historical and cultural influences that have shaped these culinary traditions, as well as the modern interpretations and adaptations. Through hands-on recipe demonstrations and virtual tours of local markets, students will gain a deeper understanding and appreciation for the complexity and nuances of international cuisines.

The Art of Plating and Food Presentation
In this course, students will learn how to visually compose and present their culinary creations. They will explore the principles of plate design, harmonious color and texture combinations, as well as impactful serving techniques.

Duration:6 Weeks

Delivery Schedule:Commencing soon...

Details:The course will cover the principles of plate composition, including balance, symmetry, and the use of negative space. Students will also delve into the strategic placement of ingredients, the play of textures and colors, and the incorporation of garnishes and decorative elements.

Healthy Eating and Nutrition
This course will introduce students to the fundamentals of healthy and balanced nutrition. They will learn about menu planning principles, preparing nutritious dishes, and receive recommendations on diets and eating for different target groups..

Duration:4 weeks

Delivery Schedule:17 September - 14 October 2024

Details:This comprehensive course will provide students with a solid foundation in the essential techniques and principles of food preparation. Participants will learn fundamental knife skills, such as dicing, slicing, and mincing, as well as mastering various cooking methods, including sautéing and baking
